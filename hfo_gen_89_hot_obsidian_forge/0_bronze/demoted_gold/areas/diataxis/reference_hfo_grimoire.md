# Reference: The 8-Port Spell Registry

This document serves as the authoritative reference for the Gen89 v1 Grimoire's 8 signature spells. Each port is assigned one primary spell that defines its core capability.

## The Registry

### P0: TRUE SEEING
* **School:** Divination 6th
* **Archetype:** Ground Truth Validator
* **Function:** Pierces illusions. Verifies that what the system reports matches what is actually happening. Catches "AI theater" and "green lies."
* **Meadows Leverage:** L6 (Information Flows)

### P1: FORBIDDANCE
* **School:** Abjuration 6th
* **Archetype:** Schema Boundary Enforcer
* **Function:** Seals areas against unauthorized travel. Ensures no data crosses a boundary without a valid contract. Fail-closed by design.
* **Meadows Leverage:** L8 (Rules)

### P2: GENESIS
* **School:** Conjuration (Epic)
* **Archetype:** Validated World Builder
* **Function:** Creates new demiplanes. Scaffolds new, self-contained workspaces from validated templates that pass all gates at birth.
* **Meadows Leverage:** L3 (Physical Structure)

### P3: GATE
* **School:** Conjuration 9th
* **Archetype:** Validated Payload Injector
* **Function:** Opens a portal for precise delivery. Injects validated artifacts into target environments with pre-flight validation and post-flight verification.
* **Meadows Leverage:** L6 (Information Flows)

### P4: WEIRD
* **School:** Illusion 9th
* **Archetype:** Adversarial Fitness Evaluator
* **Function:** Kills through phantasmal terrors. Generates realistic failure scenarios (mutation testing, red teaming) to stress-test artifacts. What survives becomes Splendor.
* **Meadows Leverage:** L5 (Negative Feedback Loops)
* **Compiled Lvl 1:** `FESTERING_ANGER`

### P5: CONTINGENCY
* **School:** Evocation 6th
* **Archetype:** Pre-Set Automated Recovery
* **Function:** Stores a spell to fire on a condition. Detects catastrophic failure and executes pre-planned resurrection without human intervention.
* **Meadows Leverage:** L9 (Self-Organization)

### P6: CLONE
* **School:** Necromancy 8th
* **Archetype:** Automated Knowledge Preservation
* **Function:** Creates an inert duplicate. Automatically ingests new artifacts into the SSOT, extracting metadata and making them searchable, ensuring no knowledge is lost to session death.
* **Meadows Leverage:** L2 (Buffers)

### P7: TIME STOP
* **School:** Transmutation 9th
* **Archetype:** Strategic Coordinator / C2 Layer
* **Function:** Freezes time to act freely. Pauses execution to audit all ports, compute optimal moves, and dispatch coordinated commands across the octave.
* **Meadows Leverage:** L10 (Goals)
* **Compiled Lvl 1:** `WISH`
